The Spider-Man

     By far the biggest Spider-man iteration to hit the big screen, Tom Holland's Spiderman is latest and current iteration of Spiderman in the movies. A huge reason for this mega-stardom is that this iteration is connected to the MCU; The home of all the Marvel superheroes in cinema. The Spider-man character was finally in it's homeworld, and it gave the character a massive boost.

     This iteration of Spider-Man is featured in over 6 different movies, over eight years! Tom's character also got a big step-up in the costumes department, as his version of the character has over 7 different suits. You can find some of his more iconic suits below:

The MCU Adventure

Spider-Man has had a long journey in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, let's take a look at the movies that he has appeared in, during his advernture in this universe:

    1. Spider-Man officially entered the MCU, in 2016, with the release of Captain Civil War.
      1. This was to the surpise of most fans, as no one had expected Spider-Man to ever in the MCU, anytime back then. 
    2. After the success of this iteration of the character, he got his own standalone movie, "Spider-Man: Homecoming". 
      1. This movie bypassed all the origin story stuff that fans had become accustomed to with the previous two iterations/franchises of the character. 
      2. It instead showed Spider-Man finding his footing as Spider-Man, and becoming the friendly neighbourhood Spider-Man everyone loves. 
      3. This movie also replaced Uncle Ben, and his Iconic line! It instead featured Tony Stark as the mentor for Peter Parker. 
  1. Following in the footsteps of Stark, Spider-Man/Peter Parker joined the Avengers, and helped in their epic-battle against Thanos, in the infinity Saga.
    1. During this battle, Peter Parker was "killed" for five years, but then was revived again afterwards. 
  2. The second Spider-Man movie, "Far From Home" was released after the "Avengers: Endgame" and dealt with the fallout from the Infinity Saga, and Peter Parker recovering from being "killed" for five years.
    1. During this movie, Peter also fought against classic Spider-Man Villain Mysterio. 
    2. The movie also followed a character arc where Peter/Spider-Man became a more independent superhero. 

This isn't the end though, the adventure for Spider-Man continues in Spider-Man: No Way Home.
