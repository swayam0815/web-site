Spider-Man: No Way Home

    The thing that EVERYTHING has been leading up to; Everything that you saw on this site has been leading you to this moment, this page right here. You see, "Spider-Man: No Way Home" is not only the third MCU Spider-Man movie, but a culmination of all the: 

"Spider-Men Across The Movies"

     Do you see it? The idea and basis for the site comes from this movie! The movie and the site both combine information and Spider-Men characters across one medium. It was the movie from where I got the idea to build this site. 

 

The Movie

   As mentioned above, this is the third Spider-Man movie in the MCU franchise. The movie marked the first time that Spider-Man got a third movie that was successful; The first and second iterations weren't lucky with their third movies. 

   The movie focused on the aftermath of the events from the first two movies of the MCU Spider-Man. Spider-Man's identity as Peter Parker gets leaked to the public, and it leads to lots of chaos and anarchy in life. In order to deal with the events, Peter Parker goes to Doctor Strange for magical help, who uses a magic spell to try and help Peter. However, like most things in a movie, the spell goes awry and instead causes MULTIVERSAL damage. 

    The side-effects from the spell cause villains from all the previous Spider-Man movies to appear in the universe where MCU Spider-Man takes place. With this, this current iteration of Peter Parker, has to fight ALL the villains that the other two Spider-Men have fought. This was a very nostalgic moment for fans who've seen all the movies, as they got to see the classic villains come back for round two. 

 

The returning villains of No Way Home.

 

The Spider-Men

   Peter wasn't alone in his battle against the villains because the same spell also brought other Peter Parkers to his Universe. This was the first time, after their franchises had been cancelled, that Tobey's and Andrew's Spiderman hit the big screen again. It also marked the first time, multiple live-action Spider-Men assembled on the screen. 

They were able to help the MCU Spider-Man with his woes, while also teaching him a thing or two about being a Spider-Man. 

 

The Conclusion

    The Spider-Men were able to defeat the villans and send them back to their respective movie-universes, but in the process the MCU Peter-Parker, lost everything he had accumulated over his past movies in the MCU. Furthermore, the other Spider-Men were also transported back to their universes, so they couldn't stick around to help this Spider-Man.
